A23Pay : Cashless Finance System

A23Pay's Cashless Finance System helps organizations accelerate their transition from physical cash handling to ‘going cashless’ with online payment collection and/or online payment processing across the mobile money platforms.
Going cashless eliminates the costs and risks of cash handling, from the workplace ranging from simple petty cash transactions, cash deposits by field workers to sizable disbursements to employees or external contractors.

Managers can perform all management functions online through the management portal. This system allows organizations to transact amounts in excess of UGX 200,000,000/- per month.
